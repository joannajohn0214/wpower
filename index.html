Did you know?



Girls are just 42% of all high school athletes.


In D1 schools, if one dollar is used on women's sports, about two dollars are used on men's.


About 40% of women face discrimination in sports.


How Can We Fix This?

Our Mission

Our job is to spread awareness on this growing issue.
We founded this organization because we were tired of experiencing it in our lives and seeing others suffer from it too.
People deserve to know the unfair treatment that female athletes are going through for only doing what they love. We as people should step up and help make a better environment for women and minorities, such as the LGBTQ, disabled, and many other races in sports. If we can all help solve this issue, we can build a better future for our youth, where they all feel included and equal.
